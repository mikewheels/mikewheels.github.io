About Us

Opening little minds at home...

Imagine your child to be open minded, in a world where more and more different cultures and nationalities are working and living together...

We are IN stands for We are International Nannies that are there for your children when you can’t be. When in need of a wonderful Nanny that cares for your child and helps him in any way that is needed, if it’s just a hug or listening to him. We will be there for him!

Our mission is to open little minds at home and make every child feel comfortable and loved. It is important for us that every child receives the special attention and care that is needed for him at any moment while their parents are not able to be there.
